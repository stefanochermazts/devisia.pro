# AI and Privacy: Data Protection & Cookie Policy

Devisia handles personal data with a focus on transparency, data minimisation and security by design. This page summarises how we collect, use and protect information when you visit the site, request information about our services or interact with tools such as contact forms, chat and downloadable resources.

## Data controller and contacts

The data controller is Devisia. For privacy requests, data subject rights or security reports, contact us via the [Contact](/en/contact) page with the subject line “Privacy” or “GDPR”.

## What data we process and why

We may process identity and contact data (name, email, company, message), technical browsing data (logs, IP address, user agent, timestamps) and cookie or consent preferences. We use this information to respond to enquiries, deliver requested services, improve the website, measure communication effectiveness and meet legal obligations.

## Legal basis and retention

Processing is based on consent, pre-contractual or contractual measures, documented legitimate interest or legal obligation, depending on context. We retain data only as long as needed for the stated purposes and in line with GDPR storage limitation principles.

## Cookies, analytics and third-party tools

We use technical, analytics and, where applicable, marketing cookies with consent management. You can change preferences at any time via the cookie banner or browser settings. The full legal policy below is generated and maintained by our policy provider.
