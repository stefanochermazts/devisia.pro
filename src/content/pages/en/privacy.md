# Privacy & Cookie Policy

This page explains how Devisia handles **personal data** and **cookies** in line with GDPR and ePrivacy requirements.

## 1) Data controller

**Devisia**  
Email: [info@devisia.pro](mailto:info@devisia.pro)

## 2) Types of data we process

- **User‑provided data**: e.g. when you submit the contact form (name, email, message).
- **Technical data**: basic logs and information required to operate the website.

## 3) Cookies and similar technologies

We use:

- **Essential cookies**: required for the website to work (always on).
- **Analytics cookies** (optional): used to measure usage in an aggregated way, only if you consent.
- **Marketing cookies** (optional): potential marketing tools, only if you consent.

## 4) Consent management

On your first visit you can:
- **Accept** all cookies
- **Reject** non‑essential cookies
- **Manage preferences** by category

You can change your choice at any time by clicking **“Preferences”** in the footer.

## 5) Legal basis

- **Essential cookies**: legitimate interest / technical necessity.
- **Non‑essential cookies (analytics/marketing)**: consent (GDPR art. 6(1)(a)).

## 6) Retention

Your consent preferences are stored for a limited period and can be updated at any time.

## 7) Your rights

You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, portability, object, and to withdraw consent at any time.

For requests: [info@devisia.pro](mailto:info@devisia.pro)
