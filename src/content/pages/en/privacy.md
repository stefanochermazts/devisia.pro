# Privacy & Cookie Policy

## 2) What data we process

We may process:

- **Contact data and content** (provided by you): name, email address, subject (optional), message content.
- **Technical and usage data**: IP address, device and browser information, timestamps, request metadata, and diagnostics required to deliver the website securely and reliably.
- **Consent preferences**: your choices about optional cookies/trackers (analytics/marketing).

We do **not** intentionally collect special categories of data (GDPR art. 9). Please avoid including sensitive personal information in free‑text fields.

## 3) Purposes and legal bases

We process personal data for the following purposes:

1. **Responding to requests / contact form submissions**  
   - **Purpose**: handle your message and reply to you.  
   - **Legal basis**: performance of pre‑contractual measures / contract (GDPR art. 6(1)(b)) and/or legitimate interest (art. 6(1)(f)).  

2. **Website security, operations, and troubleshooting**  
   - **Purpose**: protect the site, prevent abuse, and ensure service continuity.  
   - **Legal basis**: legitimate interest (GDPR art. 6(1)(f)).  

3. **Analytics and marketing (optional)**  
   - **Purpose**: measure and improve the website and/or run marketing tools.  
   - **Legal basis**: consent (GDPR art. 6(1)(a)).  
   - These technologies are **not activated** unless you opt in (where applicable).

## 4) Cookies and similar technologies

We use:

- **Essential cookies/technologies**: strictly necessary for website functionality and security (always on).
- **Analytics cookies/technologies** (optional): only if you consent.
- **Marketing cookies/technologies** (optional): only if you consent.

### Consent cookie
Your preferences are stored in a cookie named `gdpr_consent`.

- **Duration**: up to **180 days**, unless you change it earlier.
- **Content**: essential (always true), analytics (true/false), marketing (true/false).

## 5) How to manage consent

On your first visit you can:
- **Accept** all cookies
- **Reject** non‑essential cookies
- **Manage preferences** by category

You can change your choice at any time by clicking **“Preferences”** in the footer.

You can also clear the consent cookie via your browser settings. Please note that clearing cookies may reset your preferences.

## 6) Recipients and processors

We may share personal data with service providers acting as **processors** (GDPR art. 28), only as necessary to provide the website and related services (e.g. hosting, email delivery for contact replies).

We do not sell personal data.

## 7) International transfers

Some providers may process data outside the EEA/UK/CH. Where international transfers apply, we use appropriate safeguards (e.g. Standard Contractual Clauses and additional measures where required).

## 8) Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes described above:

- **Contact requests**: time needed to manage the request and any follow‑up; then stored only if needed for legal/defensive purposes.
- **Technical logs**: retained for a limited period for security and troubleshooting, then deleted or anonymized.
- **Consent cookie**: up to **180 days** (see above), unless you change it earlier.

## 9) Your rights

You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, portability, object, and to withdraw consent at any time.

For requests: [info@devisia.pro](mailto:info@devisia.pro)

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your competent supervisory authority.
